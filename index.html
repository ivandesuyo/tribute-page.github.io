Lee Chong Wei

The best badminton player in the world

Here is the story of the Malaysian legend

  • Born in Bagan Serai, Perak in 1982
  • Favoured basketball at an early age
  • Began learning badminton at the age of 11 from his father
  • Drafted into the national squad in 2000 when he was seventeen years old
  • Has a career record of 713 wins to 135 losses with 69 career titles on hand
  • Won a total of 69 BWF singles titles including 42 BWF Super Series singles titles, and 4 BWF Super Series Finals
  • The first men's singles player to have won every BWF Super Series Premier title
  • Three-time silver medalist in Olympics men's singles
  • Quadruple silver medalist at the BWF World Championships
  • Holds an astonishing career 0.66 winning rate
  • The only player to rank number one for more than 300 weeks, 349 weeks being the peak period
"If we dare to win, we should also dare to lose."
- Lee Chong Wei
